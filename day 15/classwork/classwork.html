Facebook

This article is about the social media service. For its owner, formerly known as Facebook, Inc., see Meta Platforms.
Not to be confused with Face book or The World Factbook.
Facebook is a social media and social networking service owned by the American technology
conglomerate Meta Platforms. Created in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg

History

Zuckerberg built a website called "Facemash" in 2003 while attending Harvard University.
